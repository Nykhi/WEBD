Top 10 Literature

Top 1

Oregairu

A cynical high school student is forced to join a volunteer service club, navigating complex social dynamics and relationships.

Top 2

Fate

Mages summon historical and legendary heroes to fight alongside them in a lethal battle royal for the magical Holy Grail.

Top 3

Toaru

Set in a high-tech city of espers, a boy with an ability-negating right hand crosses paths with a young nun indexing forbidden books.

Top 4

Shadow Slave

An awakened warrior struggles to survive deadly supernatural trials within a magical nightmare realm while bound by a fateful curse.

Top 5

Re:Zero

Suddenly transported to a fantasy world, an ordinary boy discovers he has the agonizing power to rewind time whenever he dies.

Top 6

Lord of the Rings

An epic high-fantasy journey following a fellowship tasked with destroying a corruptive master ring to defeat a dark lord.

Top 7

Red Rising

A miner on a strictly stratified futuristic Mars infiltrates the ruling golden elite class to spark a massive societal revolution.

Top 8

Game of Thrones

Noble families engage in brutal political betrayals, deadly warfare, and intricate schemes to claim absolute power over a continent.

Top 9

My Dress-Up Darling

An introverted doll-artisan craftsman helps a popular, outgoing classmate realize her passion for crafting detailed cosplay costumes.

Top 10

Blue Box

A wholesome sports romance tracking a badminton player's growing feelings for a star basketball player who moves into his home.
